KEY WORDS

o Venture: a new activity, usually in business, that involves risk or uncertainty.
Translation: entreprise, projet, aventure, entreprise commerciale, coup d'essai commercial.

o Broker: a person who buys and sells foreign money, shares in companies, etc., for other people.
Translation: courtier, commissionnaire, agent de change, brocanteur, commerçant.

o Estate: a large area of land in the country that is owned by a family or an organization.
Translation: biens, propriété, terre, état, fortune, ferme, condition, possessions, rang.

o Bankrupt: unable to pay what you owe, and having had control of your financial matters given, by a law court, to a person who sells your property to pay your debts.
Translation: en faillite, failli, dépourvu, faille, ruiner, mettre en faillite.

o Bankruptcy: a situation in which a business or a person becomes bankrupt.
Translation: faillite, ruine.

o Brokerage firms: an organization that buys and sells foreign money, shares in companies, etc. for other people.
Translation: sociétés de courtage.

o Portfolio: a collection of company shares and other investments that are owned by a particular person or organization.
Translation: portefeuille
